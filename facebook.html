Application removal instruction

If you want to delete your app activities for our mall app, you can remove your app activities by the following instructions.
  1. Go to Your Facebook Account's Settings & Privacy. Click Settings.
  2. Then, go to Apps and Websites and you will see all of your apps activities.
  3. Select the option box of our mall.
  4. Click Remove button.
  5. Congratulation, you are successfully remove your app activities.
